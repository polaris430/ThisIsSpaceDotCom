Colorful Clouds

The Rho Ophiuchi Complex is one of the closest — and most colorful — star-forming regions, just 360 light-years distant. This image represents 24 hours of exposure time on a 4.2-inch scope.

A Humble Bode

Bode's Galaxy (M81) is one of the prototypical spiral galaxies, lying about 12 million light-years away in Ursa Major. This LRGB image represents 7.25 hours of exposure with a 16-inch scope.

The Green Glow

Green airglow from excited oxygen atoms fills the skies over Grand Staircase-Escalante National Monument. This image is a composite mosaic; sky images had an exposure time of 15 seconds
